Meet Steve Bazen

Steve launched Bazen Woodworking after more than a decade in commercial millwork and residential finish carpentry. From Corsica, South Dakota, he leads a tight-knit crew that measures, builds, finishes, and installs custom cabinetry for kitchens, mudrooms, bathrooms, and specialty spaces across the region.

Every project follows the same hands-on rhythm: meticulous site measurements, collaborative design, in-shop fabrication, sprayed finishes, and a clean install. When you work with Steve, you work with the same team from first walkthrough to final touch-up.

The Bazen shop is outfitted with CNC-guided tooling, a dedicated finishing booth, and an installation crew that treats every home with care. Communication stays proactive—expect timeline updates, progress photos, and craftsmanship that reflects decades of experience.
